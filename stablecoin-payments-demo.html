Direct Transfer

No intermediaries. Sender's wallet debits, receiver's wallet credits in a single atomic operation. No correspondent banks, no nostro accounts, no SWIFT messages.

⚡

Instant Settlement

Finality in seconds to minutes depending on the blockchain — not 3–5 business days. The receiver can use the funds immediately upon arrival.

💎

Transparent Fees

Network fee is visible upfront before you send. No hidden deductions, no opaque FX spreads, no correspondent bank charges eating into the principal.

🌐

24/7 Availability

Blockchains don't observe banking hours, cut-off times, weekends, or holidays. Send value anytime, anywhere, to anyone with a wallet.

🏦 Minted

A regulated issuer (e.g., Circle for USDC) receives fiat deposits into a reserve account. For each dollar deposited, one stablecoin token is minted and issued on-chain. Reserves are held in cash and short-term US Treasuries, audited monthly by independent firms.

05 — What This Enables

From Days and Dollars
to Seconds and Cents

Stablecoin infrastructure unlocks new possibilities for global payments, financial inclusion, and treasury management.

💸
Remittances at Near-Zero Cost
Migrant workers sending money home lose 6–8% to fees through traditional channels. Stablecoin transfers cost fractions of a cent — putting more money in the hands of families who need it most.
⚡
Instant Global Commerce
Pay international suppliers, contractors, and freelancers in seconds. No more waiting days for wire clearance or pre-funding accounts in every country you operate in.
🌍
Financial Inclusion
2 billion people lack bank accounts but increasingly have smartphones. A stablecoin wallet gives anyone with internet access the ability to store, send, and receive US dollars — no bank required.
🏛️
Treasury Efficiency
Corporates can hold stablecoins instead of pre-funding nostro accounts in dozens of countries. Deploy capital on-demand across any corridor, earn yield on reserves until the moment of payment.
